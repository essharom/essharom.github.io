A research project with the LINK-Group at Semmelweis University examining how signaling networks reorganise during the progression from normal colon tissue to adenoma and carcinoma.

**Key contributions:**
- Gathered and analysed microarray and RNA-Seq data
- Processed data using R (affy, edgeR) and Python pipelines
