A collaborative project with the Immunoinformatics Group at the University of Edinburgh, focused on computationally generating SARS-CoV-2 spike protein variants with low immunogenic profiles for universal vaccine design.

**Key contributions:**
- Implemented and conducted the evaluation pipeline of computationally generated proteins
- Assessed structural integrity using AlphaFold2 and PyMOL
- Joint first authorship on the resulting ICML 2022 workshop paper
