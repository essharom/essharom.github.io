At Turbine.AI, I contributed to the development of network-driven cancer cell avatars — computational models that simulate cancer cell behaviour to identify effective drug combinations and predictive biomarkers for DNA damage response (DDR) inhibitors.

**Key contributions:**
- Gathered, cleaned, and analysed compound binding affinity data
- Supported drug discovery projects in collaboration with pharmaceutical partners including Bayer
- Work contributed to a US patent application for a pharmaceutical combination therapy
