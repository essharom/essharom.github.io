An ongoing research project applying graph neural networks to the identification of novel candidate genes for Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). By integrating multi-modal molecular data — including gene expression, genome-wide association studies, and biomolecular network features — we explore whether GNN-based learning frameworks can predict genes that share the properties of known ASD-associated genes.

This work builds on the [GNN-Suite](https://github.com/biomedicalinformaticsgroup/gnn-suite) benchmarking framework and represents a key application of my PhD research at the University of Edinburgh.

**Status:** Manuscript in preparation.
